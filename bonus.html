Violet Sunflower and Victor

In a world where nature and color unite, Violet Sunflower stands as a symbol of strength and wisdom. With her sidekick Victor, they bring balance to nature and light to darkness.

Powers

  • **Floral Regeneration:** Can heal injuries and regrow plants instantly.
  • **Violet Aura Shield:** Creates an energy shield for protection.

Sidekick (Victor)'s Power

  • **Petal Speed:** Can run super-fast by gliding over flower petals.
