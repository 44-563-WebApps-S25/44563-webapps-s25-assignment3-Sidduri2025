How to Make a Simple Banana Milkshake

  1. Preparing Ingredients
    1. Peel and slice the bananas.
    2. Add the banana slices to a blender.
    3. Pour in 1 cup of milk.
    4. Add honey or sugar for sweetness.
    5. Optional: Add vanilla extract for extra flavor.
    6. Add ice cubes for a chilled texture.
  2. procedure
    1. Blend everything until smooth and creamy.
    2. Pour the milkshake into a glass and serve immediately.
